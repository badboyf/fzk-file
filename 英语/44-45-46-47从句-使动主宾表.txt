046  从句-宾语从句===========================================================
跟宾语有关的句子结构
	主谓宾
	主谓间宾直宾
	主谓宾宾补
	主系表 + 从句（宾语从句） (下方括号中的约束)
宾语从句可放在
	动词后
	动词 + 名词后
	主系表后 (主语一般是人，表语是表达人感情，心情的形容词)
		i am happy that you can come 	主系表 + 宾语从句
	还可以补充一点主谓宾宾补，情况很少：i think that he can come a good thing，注意没有is

宾语从句的时态： 主过从过,如果主是现在或将来，从句该是什么就是什么
i knew that he went to BJ (he goes to beijing)
i knew that he had gone to BJ (he has gone to beijing)
i knew that he would go to BJ (he will go to BJ)
i knew that he was watching TV (he is watching TV)
i knew that he had been watching TV (he has been watching TV)

he told me that 主谓间宾直宾
Did he say that he would come tomorrow	主谓
why didn't you tell him you were a teacher 主谓宾
i am angry that you didn't tell me 	主系表，表语表示情绪
are you angry that he didn't you	他没告诉你你生气吗

否定前移
我觉得他是个幽默的人	i think that he is a humous person
我觉得他不是个幽默的人	
否定前移的词，think，believe

疑问词前移
你觉得他会去哪了呢		do you think where you went -> where do you think he went
你觉得这个房子多大呢	how big do you think the house is

一般用过去时
我没想到你会来			i didn't expect that you would came
knew, doubt, worried, thought

if/wether	都是三种宾语从句
i don't know wether he is a teacher	动词后面
i am not sure wether he will come 	主系表后
he asked me if i could help him 	动词加名词后，主谓间宾直宾

疑问词 + to do 有条件
	1从句和主句主语是一样的
	2还未发生
i don't know how to tell him the thing
we don't know where to go

主谓宾宾补
i think that he can come a good thing(注意不加is)	-> i think it a good thing he can come
